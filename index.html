About

Located conveniently between Oak Island and Southport on Long Beach Road, CCBC will have something for everyone - whether you love to try the newest thing or stick with the tried and true.
The 18-acre property offers the perfect setting for live music, food trucks, and events. Come enjoy the secluded park-like atmosphere, just minutes from the beach and historic downtown Southport.
Brewmaster Justin "Maggs" Maggard brings years of experience as a passionate and creative brewer, as well as a true love of good beer. We can't wait to raise a glass with you!
